GROUNDWATER MODELLING

  • Software : QGIS (Georeferencing and Pre-modelling work), GMS(MODFLOW and MT3DMS).
  • Importance of GW-modelling : Once a model has been demonstrated to reasonably reproduce past behavior, they can forecast the outcome of future groundwater behavior, support decision-making and allow the exploration of alternative management approaches.
  • Types of modelling done : Contaminant transport(Using nitrate as contaminant) and GW flow. Area under study - Ain river, France.
  • Work done : Predicted groundwater levels throughout the region using MODFLOW(GMS) and predicted transport of nitrate contaminant in the region over various time periods (via graphs and video simulations using MT3DMS(GMS).)
